Top three activities to do at Xiamen

Visit Gulangyu Island

Known for its stunning island scenery and colonial architecture, Gulangyu is a peaceful place to stroll, with highlights like the Piano Museum.

Try Local Cuisine

Xiamen is famous for its seafood and snacks. Don't miss dishes like Shacha Noodles especially around Zhongshan Road or Eight Markets.

Explore Nanputuo Temple

Visit this historic Buddhist temple, often called one of China's most picturesque temple.
